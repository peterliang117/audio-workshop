# Audio Workshop iOS (Personal MVP)

## Scope (must follow)
- Personal audio editing + export utility.
- Audio sources: Files/iCloud/Share Sheet imports only.
- No YouTube downloading/scraping features.

## MVP Features
1) Import audio (Files/iCloud)
2) Preview playback with scrubber
3) Basic edits applied at export-time:
   - Trim (start/end)
   - Volume (0.0 ~ 2.0)
   - Fade in/out (0~5s)
4) Export:
   - Audio: m4a (default), wav (optional)
   - Video: black-screen mp4 (1080x1920 portrait, 30fps)

## Export Defaults
- Black video size: 1080x1920
- FPS: 30
- Container: .mp4
- Video: H.264
- Audio: AAC
